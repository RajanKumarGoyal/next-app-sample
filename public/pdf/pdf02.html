Compass Marine Medical B.V and its associated companies provide a full audit service, for all types of ships and off-shore installations. It is essential for all ocean going vessels to carry the correct medical and pharmaceutical products to ensure safety and compliance whilst at sea. We carry out both physical and remote inspections of your medical facility. Continued monitoring of your medicals supplies ensures that your medical chest is complete and up to date with your specific flag state or medical requirement.

This Certificate of Compliance has been issued based on a physical inspection or a complete audit of your medical inventory and includes required quantities, expiry dates and condition of your equipment and first aid kits. The certificate is valid from the specified date show overleaf and is for the vessel named with the IMO number as shown. The certificate runs for one year from the date of issue, or until a new certificate is issued. Certificates are not transferable. In the event the vessel is re-flagged a new certificate will be issued based on the changed registration and medical requirement.

All information provided by the vessel, management company or ship owners will be taken as accurate and complete. In the event of inventory records provided be incomplete or inaccurate, this certificate will be void and new certificate will need to be requested.

In the event that an order is required to bring the vessels medical chest into compliance, the order will be delivered to the next convenient port call and a certificate will be issued with the order. Electronic copies can be emailed in the event that the ship requires a replacement due to the original being lost or damaged.

The Master of the vessel is ultimately responsible to ensure that all information provided is correct and that the ships medical scales are complete.
